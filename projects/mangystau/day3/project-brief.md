# Mangystau Day 3 Project Brief

## Project

**Title:** Day 3 — Layers of Color and The Fangs  
**Series:** Mangystau Documentary  
**Destination:** Mangystau, Kazakhstan  
**Target Duration:** 4 minutes  
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9  
**Output Format:** Documentary sequence, silent video with broadcast-style subtitles

---

## Route

Yurt → Zhanaozen Supply Stop → Kyzylkup Tiramisu Canyon → Bokty Mountain → Bozzhyra Lower Camp → The Fangs Sunset Trek

---

## Restrictions

- Do not generate narration audio.
- Do not generate background music.
- Do not generate sound effects.
- Use cinematic broadcast-style subtitles only.
- Use drone footage heavily during narration gaps.
- Avoid travel-vlog style.
- Avoid flashy transitions.
- Avoid YouTube-style captions.

---

## Creative Direction

**Style:** Netflix Documentary / BBC Earth / National Geographic  
**Mood:** vast, quiet, geological, cinematic, existential  
**Core Emotion:** entering deeper wilderness, discovery, awe, smallness under the universe  
**Ending Feeling:** human smallness and cosmic depth

---

## Required Visual Priorities

### Zhanaozen

- Road drone
- Vehicle movement
- Supply loading
- Last city / town edge
- Empty road leaving civilization

### Kyzylkup

- Drone reveal
- Layered hills
- Top-down geological texture
- Red and white layers
- Sphinx rock if available
- Light variation across the canyon

### Bokty

- Distant approach
- Wide isolated mountain
- Orbit drone
- Human or vehicle scale if available
- Pull-back ending

### Bozzhyra / The Fangs

- Lower camp
- Tent setup
- Golden hour waiting
- Walking silhouette
- The Fangs reveal
- Massive limestone towers
- Sunset / blue hour
- Milky Way / night sky
- Long final hero shot

---

## Director Intention

This episode should feel like moving beyond the last human settlement into a geological world where color, time, and scale become the main characters.

The ending must not feel like a simple scenic conclusion. It must feel existential: the viewer should feel small under the universe.
