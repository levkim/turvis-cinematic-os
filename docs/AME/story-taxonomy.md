# Story Taxonomy

Version: v0.1  
Project: TURVIS Adventure Memory Engine

---

## Purpose

Story tags describe how a clip can function inside a documentary sequence.

They answer:

> What job can this clip do in the story?

---

## Core Story Tags

### Opening

A clip strong enough to introduce a film or chapter.

Usually:

- Hero landscape
- Drone reveal
- Wide establishing shot
- Strong atmosphere

### Establishing

A clip that tells the viewer where they are.

Usually:

- Wide landscape
- town edge
- road and destination context
- aerial geography

### Threshold

A clip that shows leaving one world and entering another.

Usually:

- road leaving city
- vehicle crossing empty land
- desert entrance
- mountain pass

### Journey

A clip that carries movement between places.

Usually:

- vehicle tracking
- road drone
- walking shot
- dust trail

### Preparation

A clip that shows getting ready.

Usually:

- supply loading
- gear checking
- tent packing
- fuel stop

### Discovery

A clip that reveals a new place or important subject.

Usually:

- drone reveal
- slow push
- first view
- turn around corner

### Texture

A clip that explains surface, geology, snow, rock, water, sand, or detail.

Usually:

- top-down drone
- close landscape detail
- lateral movement across layers

### Scale

A clip that communicates size.

Usually:

- tiny human
- small vehicle
- high drone pull-back
- large landscape with reference point

### Arrival

A clip that shows reaching a meaningful place.

Usually:

- camp arrival
- first view of landmark
- final road approach

### Camp

A clip that shows temporary home inside wilderness.

Usually:

- tent setup
- stove
- fire
- camp under large sky

### Reflection

A quiet clip that allows emotional pause.

Usually:

- sunset
- still human figure
- camp at blue hour
- slow landscape hold

### Ending

A clip strong enough to close a film emotionally.

Usually:

- night sky
- hero pull-back
- final wide
- long silent landscape

---

## Usage Rule

Each clip should receive 1–3 story tags.

Story tags should describe how the clip can be used, not only what the clip contains.
